What is it?

In recent years, the bee population has been at a sharp decline. Scientists have named the phenomenon "colony collapse disorder" (CCD). Dramatic events have occurred, ridding the world of its bees: there has been a loss of 30% from beekeepers in the past 10 years, in both the United States and Europe. In addition, 44 percent of beehives collapsed April 2015-16, which is three times the acceptable amount of beehives gone. This phenomenon is of serious concern to many scientists, who fear a tragic ending to this dramatic event.
